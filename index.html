The idea

One message. A whole back office goes to work.

Sourcing, number-crunching, chasing agents, keeping track — it eats a dealer's day. The Firm hands each of those jobs to a dedicated agent. You stay the boss: you set the rules, you make the call, you press send. They do the legwork.

The team

Four agents, one chat

Each has a single job and does it well. Talk to them in plain English — or a voice note.

🔎

Scout

Sourcing

Reads any property you feed it — a pasted link, a listing, or a forwarded estate-agent email — and pulls out the facts that matter: address, price, condition, tenure, the lot.

📊

Analyst

Underwriting

The engine. Pulls real sold prices from HM Land Registry, works out resale value, refurb cost and your true profit — then gives a verdict and the maximum you should pay.

🤝

Negotiator

Comms

Drafts your offers, agent emails, chasers and counter-offers — leading with your edge as a cash, chain-free buyer. It writes; you copy, check and send. It never reveals your ceiling.

🗂️

Clerk

Tracking

Keeps every deal moving through your pipeline, remembers the details, sets reminders, and sends you a morning briefing so nothing slips.

How it works

From a link to a decision in seconds

01

Send a property

Paste a link, type an address with a price, or forward an agent's email into the chat. A voice note works too.

02

The team works it

Scout reads it, the Analyst underwrites it against real local sold prices and your buy-box, the Clerk files it.

03

You get a verdict

A clear scorecard: buy or walk, the max to pay, profit and ROI — with the comparables behind it. You decide.

Built for the way you deal

A cash buyer's edge, baked in

The Firm is designed around how you actually operate — fast, in cash, on your terms — not a generic property app.

  • ✓Cash & chain-free first. Every offer leads with your speed and certainty — the reason agents take you seriously.
  • ✓Halal by design. No mortgages, no interest, no riba — the model assumes cash throughout.
  • ✓You always decide. The team recommends and drafts; it never sends or commits anything on its own.
  • ✓Real, free data. Valuations rest on official HM Land Registry sold prices — not guesswork.
  • ✓Private & locked. Your bot answers only to you. Nobody else can use it.
